# Discovery Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Business problem is documented.
- [ ] Sponsor or request owner is identified.
- [ ] Key stakeholders are listed.
- [ ] Current-state process is understood at a working level.
- [ ] Assumptions and constraints are recorded.
- [ ] Major risks and dependencies are visible.
- [ ] Scope boundaries are drafted.
- [ ] Next elicitation activities are scheduled.
