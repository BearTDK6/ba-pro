# BA Delivery Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Approved scope is traceable to requirements.
- [ ] High-priority requirements have acceptance criteria.
- [ ] Open decision items are visible.
- [ ] UAT plan is agreed.
- [ ] Test scenarios cover primary business flows.
- [ ] Release impacts are documented.
- [ ] Training or communication needs are identified.
- [ ] Residual risks are known and accepted.
