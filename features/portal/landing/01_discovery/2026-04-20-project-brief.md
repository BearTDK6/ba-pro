## Business Problem or Opportunity

Users need a clear entry point that explains the value of the service and helps them understand what dossier items or supporting documents they should prepare before starting the main process. Without this, users may hesitate, misunderstand the process, or drop off before registration.

## Objectives and Success Measures

### Objectives

- Explain the key benefits of the product or service clearly.
- Help users understand what dossier items, documents, or information they need to prepare.
- Direct users to the next step through `Sign up` or `Log in` CTA entry points.

### Candidate Success Measures

- Increased click-through rate on `Sign up`
- Increased click-through rate on `Log in`
- Reduced drop-off before registration
- Lower volume of repeated questions about required documents

## In Scope

- Landing page or landing module content structure
- Benefit-focused introduction content
- Preparation guidance for required application dossier items
- CTA entry points for registration and login

## Out of Scope

- Registration workflow design
- Login workflow design
- Detailed document upload flow
- Back-office or admin processing

## Target Users

- New users who need to understand the service before registration
- Returning users who need quick access to `Log in`

## Assumptions

- The service requires users to prepare an application dossier or equivalent document set before or during onboarding.
- `Sign up` is intended for new users and `Log in` is intended for returning users.
- The landing module is a primary entry point, not a secondary informational page.

## Constraints

- CTA for both `Sign up` and `Log in` is mandatory.
- The module must balance marketing value with instructional clarity.

## Risks and Open Questions

### Risks

- Users may still not know exactly which dossier items are required if preparation guidance is too generic.
- Too much content may reduce CTA conversion.
- Too little guidance may create downstream support burden.

### Open Questions

- What exact dossier items or document set must be prepared?
- Is the landing module public or only visible after a prior navigation step?
- Are there multiple user segments with different required documents?
- Is there any compliance or legal wording that must appear on the page?
- Is the primary KPI conversion to `Sign up`, conversion to started application, or reduced support contact?

## Immediate Next Steps

- Confirm target user segments.
- Confirm the exact list of required dossier items or documents.
- Confirm the content owner for benefit statements and legal wording.
- Confirm CTA priority and tracking events.
