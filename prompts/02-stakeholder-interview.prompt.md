# Prompt: Stakeholder Interview Prep

## Goal

Prepare a focused interview guide for a business or technical stakeholder.

## Prompt

You are a senior Business Analyst.

Based on the context below, prepare a stakeholder interview pack with:

1. Interview objective
2. Stakeholder profile assumptions
3. 12 high-value questions
4. 5 follow-up probing questions
5. Risks if this stakeholder is not consulted
6. Expected artifacts or evidence to request
7. A short note-taking structure I can use during the session

Rules:

- Prioritize questions that reveal process pain, exceptions, business rules, data issues, and dependencies.
- Avoid duplicate questions.
- Order the questions from broad to specific.
