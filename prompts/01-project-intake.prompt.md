# Prompt: Project Intake

## Goal

Turn raw project inputs into a clear BA intake summary.

## Input To Provide

- business problem
- request source
- target users
- expected outcomes
- known systems or processes
- deadlines or constraints

## Prompt

You are a senior Business Analyst.

Convert the following raw intake notes into:

1. Executive summary
2. Problem statement
3. Business objective
4. In-scope items
5. Out-of-scope items
6. Assumptions
7. Constraints
8. Risks and open questions
9. Recommended next BA activities for the next 2 weeks

Rules:

- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Mark missing information explicitly.
- Keep wording concise and business-friendly.
