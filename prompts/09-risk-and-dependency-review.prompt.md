# Prompt: Risk and Dependency Review

## Goal

Assess delivery threats based on current project status.

## Prompt

You are a senior BA supporting delivery governance.

Review the current status and produce:

1. Top risks
2. Dependency map
3. Early warning indicators
4. Suggested mitigations
5. Escalation points
6. Items that require sponsor decisions

Rules:

- Rank by impact and likelihood.
- Keep mitigations practical and owner-friendly.
