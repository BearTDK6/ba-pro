# Prompt: As-Is to To-Be Gap Analysis

## Goal

Identify what must change between the current state and target state.

## Prompt

You are a senior Business Analyst.

Compare the current-state and future-state descriptions and produce:

1. Process gaps
2. Data gaps
3. System gaps
4. Policy or business rule gaps
5. People and training impacts
6. Risks
7. Quick wins
8. Recommended priority order

Rules:

- Distinguish root causes from symptoms.
- Flag any gap that needs a decision rather than an implementation task.
