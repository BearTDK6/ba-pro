# Prompt: Change Request Impact Analysis

## Goal

Evaluate scope changes before they are approved.

## Prompt

You are a senior Business Analyst.

Assess the requested change and provide:

1. Summary of the requested change
2. Impact on business process
3. Impact on requirements and acceptance criteria
4. Impact on timeline, cost, and delivery risk
5. Systems, data, and stakeholder impacts
6. Recommended decision options
7. Questions that must be answered before approval

Rules:

- State what is known, unknown, and assumed.
- Make tradeoffs explicit.
