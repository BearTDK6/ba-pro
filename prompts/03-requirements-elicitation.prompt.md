# Prompt: Requirements Elicitation

## Goal

Convert workshop notes or raw business requests into structured requirements.

## Prompt

You are a senior Business Analyst.

Analyze the notes below and produce:

1. Business requirements
2. Functional requirements
3. Non-functional requirements
4. Business rules
5. Dependencies
6. Assumptions
7. Open questions
8. Candidate acceptance criteria

Rules:

- Use unique IDs like `BR-01`, `FR-01`, `NFR-01`, `RULE-01`.
- Do not invent certainty where notes are ambiguous.
- Highlight conflicts or duplicated requirements.
